Meet the Speaker

Dr. Abdulhameed Babatunde Owolabi

Researcher Professor - Smart Control & Intelligent Systems Lab
Kyungpook National University, South Korea.

In Brief

A building energy analyst that has a passion for building energy performance through the use of renewable energy technologies and energy efficiency measures to enhance the consumption of less water, minimize waste production, increase energy efficiency, and create a healthy living for the sustainable development of the eco-systems. Also, a motivated clean energy analyst that has a passion for the decarbonization of energy systems through the use of renewable energy technologies and energy efficiency measures for the sustainable development of cities across the globe.

Achievements

  • 1. Consulting for the Global Green Growth Institute (GGGI) Seoul Korea on building energy performance assessment of retrofitted.

  • 2. One of the 120 researchers from 18 nations across the globe working in collaboration with Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (Berkeley Lab), United State of America (USA) on Cool Building Solutions for a Warming World (CBS). Saddled with the mission to identify, assess, develop, and share passive and low-energy building energy efficiency measures that help individuals and cities adapt to extreme heat.

  • 3. Collaborating with Prof Gloria Pignatta at the Faculty of Built Environment, University of New South Wales (UNSW) Australia on the assessment of energy consumption for energy use and energy conservation/production strategies on apartment buildings in Australia. The research result obtained will be used to apply for the 2021 Australian Research Council Linkage grant in December 2021.

  • 4. Collaborating with Prof Jeong Soo Huh, Institute for Global Climate Change and Energy, Kyungpook National University, Daegu Korea. The research is centered on developing retrofitting measures that can be adapted by residential apartments in Korea after energy audits.
